While it's important to choose a vehicle that's affordable to buy and insure, a first car should also fit the driver's wants if possible. For basic all-around transportation, vehicles like the Toyota RAV4 and Honda CR-V can be had for good prices and shouldn't be too hard to insure, and they offer immense practicality, reliability, and decent gas mileage. For more fun and savings with a little less interior space, the Honda Fit has proven to be an excellent all-around vehicle, featuring dynamics good enough to start in its own amateur racing series and enough practicality to make for a perfect daily driver.
